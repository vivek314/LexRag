Why LexRAG is Better (Explain Like I'm 5)

Imagine you are trying to find an answer inside a giant stack of books. Here is why LexRAG wins every single time:

📖

1. Page-Boundary Blindness

Baseline RAG blindly cuts sentences right in half across page folds, which loses the meaning of the words! LexRAG respects pages and keeps paragraph thoughts whole.

🗺️

2. Spanning Questions

If an answer starts on page 3 and finishes on page 4, standard RAG only gives you page 3. LexRAG reads a page and automatically looks at its next-door neighbors (±1 page) so it gets the whole story!

💡

3. Vocabulary Mismatch

If you ask "what is the cost?", but the book uses the word "monetary penalty", normal search fails. LexRAG uses HyDE to imagine the answer first, aligning the vocabulary instantly.

📍

4. Grounded Citations

Standard RAG loses page numbers and says your answer is somewhere on page -1 (unknown!). LexRAG points to the exact PDF page, so you can open the book and verify it yourself instantly!

Critical for Legal & Medical Documents: Legal statutes (like IRS regulations) and medical dossiers contain deeply dense tables, cross-references ("see Section 46"), and statements spanning multiple pages. Standard naive RAG fails completely on these because it breaks structure. LexRAG is designed from the ground up to solve these structural issues.
